### Tone by Context
| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Success | [Celebratory/Affirming] | "You're all set!" |
| Error | [Helpful/Not blaming] | "We couldn't save. Try again?" |
| Warning | [Clear/Not alarming] | "This action can't be undone" |
| Loading | [Patient/Reassuring] | "Loading your data..." |
| Empty state | [Helpful/Actionable] | "No results. Try a different search" |

## Writing Guidelines

### Headlines
- Max [X] characters
- Action-oriented
- No periods

### Body Copy
- Short sentences
- Active voice
- One idea per paragraph

### Button Labels
- Verb-first: "Save changes" not "Changes saved"
- Specific: "Create account" not "Submit"
- Max 3 words

### Error Messages
1. Say what happened
2. Say why (if helpful)
3. Say how to fix

Example:
> "Email already registered. Try logging in or use a different email."

## Formatting

### Dates & Times
- Dates: Jan 15, 2024 (not 01/15/24)
- Times: 3:00 PM (not 15:00)
- Relative: "2 hours ago" for < 24 hours

### Numbers
- Spell out one through nine
- Use numerals for 10+
- Currency: $100, $1,000

### Capitalization
- Headlines: Title Case
- Buttons: Sentence case
- Labels: Sentence case
